what I learned

I learned that advocating for diversity, inclusivity, and cultural paradigm shift will be a large part of my legacy and brand. I learned there are many more advocates for diversity from many different backgrounds than I realize. I learned that the conversation hasn't collectively been started yet, and I'm excited to be an advocate and to represent my demographic(s).

questions I have

Where else can we start this conversation? How can I personally start this conversation? Who are my own advocates? Who can I be an advocate for?
